About Us

We are a group of Paramotor pilots who want to share our experience of flight, and grow our sport.

We started Utah Paramotor 10 years ago, and have trained hundreds of pilots from all around the country, we feel that everyone should experience the breath taking, and magnificent views only birds are able have.
There is no greater feeling than the feeling of flight your on your own, you make the decisions and your experience will always be different than the next persons.

There is no better time to try something new than now, we promise you will not regret your decision whether that be learning to fly or taking a tandem flight.
